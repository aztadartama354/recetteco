<h3>Ingrédients de porridge banane :</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>Préparer 50 g flocon avoine. </li>
	
		<li>Fournir 1 cs sirop erable. </li>
	
		<li>Préparer 200 ml lait amande. </li>
	
		<li>Préparer 1 demi banane ecrasee. </li>
	
</ol>

I loved it so much that I watched my mom make it and mimicked her. Several years later, I still love this stuff, it&#39;s a hearty breakfast or evening treat! The porridge is ready when the texture is creamy. Serve warm with some Haitian Bread.
